What The Hell is a Smore's Poptart

The Smore's Poptart is one of the worlds succulent, delicate, and flavorful ooey gooey pastries of all time. Known for it's convienient packaging, elegant yet simplistic shape, and flakey yet buttery texture, the Smore's Poptart makes for one of the most delicious snacks one could indulge in. The chocolate frosting, the graham cracker crust, the marshmallowey and chocolatey insides, every element combines to make the delectable, Smore's Poptart.
